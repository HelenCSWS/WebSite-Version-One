Hong Kong Sales Management

Hong Kong has a population of approximately 7.5 million people and is the 4th highest population density in the world. This market is currently seen as the most competitive wine market in the world today with virtually no government regulation or taxes on Wine or Beer. It also acts as a gateway for brokers to move large quantities of wine into mainland China. While this market is a vexing one to enter for Producers Christopher Stewart focuses on proper brand building, has a full team based in the market, provides 24 hour turnaround for customer deliveries, and sells only to on and off premise accounts.

Christopher Stewart Wine & Spirits
Macau Sales Management

Macau has a relatively small population of approximately 650,000 people but it is the gaming capital of Asia receiving almost 31 million visitors annually. It also has the world’s highest population density. The opportunities for Wine and Spirit sales are high through the Casino Hotels and on premise accounts. In 2018 a bridge linking Hong Kong directly to Macau will be completed making distribution much more efficient. Christopher Stewart services the market from our Hong Kong sales team.

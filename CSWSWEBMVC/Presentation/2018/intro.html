Christopher Stewart Wine & Sprits
Executive Management

Christopher Stewart Wine & Sprits Inc. was founded in 2001 by the executive team of Chris Purcell and Garry Preiswerck. Prior to establishing Christopher Stewart, Chris and Garry came from Top Producer Systems, North America’s #1 Real Estate Sales and Marketing software company of over 400+ employees.

Chris Purcell was the co-founder and Executive Vice President responsible for managing the North American sales force and for all software development initiatives. Garry Preiswerck was the Vice President of Development and managed a development group of over 200 people. In the year 2000 they sold the company to a NASDAQ publicly traded firm and decided to pursue their passion in the wine industry. With over 25 years’ experience working together and building Top Producer System to over 400+ employees, Chris and Garry bring one of the most seasoned and experienced management teams to the Canadian liquor industry today.

Producer Representation and Selection

Christopher Stewart’s guiding principal for representation is to work with “best in class” products. Whether it’s wine, spirits, sake or beer, all of these can be superlative in their own right and exhibit unique qualities when responsibly enjoyed.

We are committed to bringing to market producers whose products are of a consistent high quality and which best represent their categories.
